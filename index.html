Mahendra Singh Dhoni

He is a Former Indian and also CSK Team captain.

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017, and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.

Books and Teaching:

  • In 2021 and 2022, Karen Levy and I taught a new course entitled Choices and Consequences in Computing (INFO 1260 / CS 1340). The course is designed at an introductory level with no formal prerequisites, and covers a range of ethical, societal, and policy implications of computing and information.

  • J. Kleinberg, E. Tardos. Algorithm Design. Addison Wesley, 2005.
